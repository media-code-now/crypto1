About ICO

An Initial Coin Offering (ICO) is the cryptocurrency space's rough equivalent to an IPO in the mainstream investment world. This term refers to the creation and sale of digital tokens.

ICOs act as fundraisers of sorts; a company looking to create a new coin, app, or service launches an ICO. Next, interested investors buy in to the offering. In exchange for their support, investors receive a new cryptocurrency token specific to the ICO. The token may provide a stellar return on investment.

Tokenization Benefits

Top 3 advantages of tokenization
  • Tokenization protects sensitive information from those connected to your organization.
  • It is easier for merchants to become PCI compliant with tokenization as retailers don’t store any data.
  • Tokenization not only works with credit card plastic, but it also works with gift cards, NFC payments, ACH transfers etc.

Problems & Solutions

Problem
Missing Funds
  • Funds being stolen is perhaps the largest issue with ICOs as they usually draw in many investors.
  • Investing in an unreviewed ICO is quite risky.
  • ICOs are currently vulnerable to misrepresentation, fraud and manipulation.
Solution
ICO Assessment
  • To avoid and minimize risks, ICO review & assessment procedures are necessary.
  • It should cover both technical and legal sides.
  • The assessment is set-up like a quality seal for investors, giving them the needed security.
Problem
Data Leaks
  • Data leaks and phishing attempts have become a constant threat during ICOs.
  • Low security level is also a major issue.
  • There is a shortage of ICO protection solutions that are proactive in identifying new threats.
Solution
Better Protection
  • Investing in ICOs through Get Unit ensures your data is safe and well-protected.
  • AI & smart contracts guarantee better protection.
  • Scan your internal network for threats and vulnerabilities with Get Unit’s solutions.
Problem
Lack of transparency
  • The providers of ICOs are often not transparent when it comes to the information they provide.
  • Sometimes, no information is provided at all.
  • This lack of transparency is also an obstacle to efficient pricing of the tokens.
Solution
Accurate information
  • Get Unit ICO is always ready to provide any kind of essential information to investors.
  • All facts and information are double-checked.
  • Such an approach allows to establish full transparency and improve user experience.
